https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/vuppalaadithyasairam/bone-fracture-detection-using-xrays

This dataset comprises of fractured and non-fractured x-ray images of several joints. The task is to build an image classifier to detect fractures in given x-ray image.
This dataset comprises of different joints in the upper extremities, isolation of individual joints is recommended for enhanced performance of classifiers.
